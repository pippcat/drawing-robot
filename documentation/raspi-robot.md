## Specs of the motor

- MG90S:
 - Weight: 13.4 g
 - Dimension: 22.5 x 12 x 35.5

 - Operating voltage: 4.8 V - 6.0V
 - Dead band width: 5 μs
 - PWM period: 50 Hz / 20ms
 - duty cycle: 1-2ms (1ms = -90°; 1,5ms = 0°; 2ms = 90°)
